A-Level Graphics

Between 2020 and 2022 I took A-Level Graphics at college, in this course i managed to achieve an A* with my graphics project that took iconic Studio Ghibli characters and movies and turned them into posters and book covers inspired by other artists. I started with lots of experiments and finished with three final pieces.

Experiments

I started my project with some quick, rough concept art, I find for any project that you start its good to have a sense and feel of what you are going into. Here I took images from the internet, merged them together and painted over them this really helped in solidifying the idea for the project.

I tried many different styles before ending up on a final one, all these were made quickly so i could find a style i liked.

Pretty much all of these started with a quick sketch and a bit of inspiration from another artist, in the end I sort of mixed all the styles together to create these final pieces.
